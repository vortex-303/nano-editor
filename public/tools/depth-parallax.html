How it works

  1. Add a photo.
  2. A local model estimates its depth map, and a WebGL shader animates the parallax. Tune the strength live.
  3. Export a frame-exact MP4, WebM, or GIF.

Why Nano Editor

  • Private by design. The AI runs on your device with WebGPU — your images never leave your computer.
  • Actually free. No account, no subscription, no watermark, no per-image cost.
  • Part of a full studio. This tool is one node in a visual, node-based image editor — chain it with background removal, upscaling, generation and more.
  • Open source. MIT-licensed on GitHub.

FAQ

What formats can I export?

MP4 (H.264), WebM (VP9), or animated GIF — all rendered frame-exact in the browser.

Do I need a depth map?

No — depth is computed automatically with Depth Anything V2. You can also feed your own.

Is it free and private?

Yes. Everything runs locally; your photo is never uploaded.
